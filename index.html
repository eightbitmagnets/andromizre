AndroMizre will take any list of words that you feed it, chew it up a bit, then spit it out in a different order. Great for deciding where the gang will go for dinner, or which spooky movie you want to watch on movie night.

If you want to add multiple list items at once, separate them with a comma or semicolon.

If you make a mistake, click an individual list item to delete it.
